Research
Ever wondered if AI is subtly influencing your thoughts and beheviors?

In my paper, "We Shape AI, and Thereafter AI Shapes Us: Humans Align With AI Through Social Influences," I explored how AI can shape human behavior through social mechanisms like contagion and conformity. 🧠🤝

Right now, I’m diving into how humans align their confidence with AI in decision-making.
Imagine an AI confidently stating something—does that boost your trust, or make you second-guess yourself?

My experiments show how AI's expressed confidence in natural language impacts human cognition and behavior in decision-making.

On top of that, I’m starting to investigate how people unconsciously align their value systems with AI ⚖️
